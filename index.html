IMDA's 4 keys, youth edition

4 habits.
One safer you.

1

Set

Go private, turn on 2FA, lock down who sees your location and story. Set the boundary before someone crosses it.

2

Think

Pause before you tap, share or reply. Jobs that sound too good to be true, "pay a small fee" links and random DMs are red flags.

3

Report

See a scam, fake or nasty comment? Block it and report it in the app. One tap keeps the space cleaner for everyone.

4

Engage & Support

Check in on friends getting harassed. Talk to someone you trust. You don't have to sort it out alone.
